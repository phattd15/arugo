Help & About

What is this site ?

This site helps users that want to do practice and can keep track of their progress through virtual rating system. You can assign yourself with initial rating, do practice based on random suggested problem and earn virtual rating through it. This site is inspired by my past training day, which I always wanted to have something like this to aid my training.

How do I register ?

First you must have a codeforces account. Then, to register for this site you need to show the ownership of the account by submitting a compile error solution to 1302I. Please make sure that by the time you submit the request, CE is the last submission. The process is very similar to TLE discord bot.

How to submit a compile error ?

You can submit a nonsense solution in c++ to that problem.

How to take a challenge ?

First, go to the "list" section and pick up a problem of your preference. You can take a brief look at the problem through the link and can decide whether to take it or not (though I recommend to always take it without peeking). After that, the problem list will be locked and you will have 80 minutes to finish the challenge. If you have the problem accepted, please refresh or reaccess the challenge site to validate your solution. Please make sure that the last submission is also the accepted one. Or else, when 80 minutes mark passed and you could not solve it or you gave up, your rating will get reduced.

What is my rating progress ?

Your rating progress is the initial rating when you registered this account. And by taking challenges, your virtual rating will change and you can view the last 30 changes through the graph.

I forgot my password or want to change the rating level ?

Do the same as in the registration process. The site will update your new infos.
